Privacy Policy

Last Updated: March 31, 2026

Your privacy is our priority. StickItInApp is designed to be a high-utility battery management tool that respects your data sovereignty.

1. Data Collection

We do NOT collect, transmit, or share any personal information. All app data, including battery history, charging speed logs, and custom sound selections, is stored locally on your device in secure storage (UserDefaults).

2. Third-Party Services

The app does not use any third-party analytics, tracking, or advertising SDKs. Your device usage stays on your device.

3. Cloud Storage

We do not use cloud storage or external servers. Your historical battery trends are only accessible through the app on your physical device.

4. Contact Us
