Renewal and cancellation

  • Payment is charged to your Apple ID when you confirm purchase.
  • Subscriptions renew automatically each week unless cancelled at least 24 hours before the end of the current period.
  • Your account may be charged for renewal within 24 hours before the end of the current period.
  • You can manage or cancel at any time in Settings > Apple ID > Subscriptions.
  • Access continues until the end of the paid period after cancellation.
  • If a free trial or introductory offer is available, unused trial time may be forfeited when you purchase a subscription.

Restore purchases

Use the Restore Purchases option inside Penpal AI to refresh eligible subscription access and previous StoreKit purchases.

Consumable boosts and voice credits

Penpal AI also sells one-time consumable purchases. These are not subscriptions and do not automatically renew.

  • Voice Credits: 25 credits (£2.99), 75 credits (£6.99), or 175 credits (£14.99).
  • Chat Boosts: extra premium chat tokens used after plan allowance.
  • Image Boosts: extra image generations used after plan image allowance.
  • Consumables are applied to your account balance after purchase verification and may be subject to fair-use, security, and abuse-prevention controls.
