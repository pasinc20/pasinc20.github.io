Quick answers

Minimum requirements

  • iOS/iPadOS 16 or later recommended
  • Microphone & Speech Recognition permissions enabled
  • Internet for AI “Go!” (Gemini); offline speech depends on iOS language packs

Where is my data stored?

On your device only. Transcripts, Saved items, and Game Plans are saved in the app’s sandbox (Documents & Application Support). There is no developer cloud for your content.

How do I delete my data?

  • Swipe to delete individual items in Saved or Game Plans.
  • Use “Clear All” in Saved to remove queued transcripts.
  • Uninstalling the app removes all data in the container.

Troubleshooting

“Tap to record” doesn’t capture my voice

  • Open iOS Settings → Privacy & Security → Microphone and enable ThinkTask.
  • Open Settings → Siri & Search → Siri & Dictation and ensure Dictation is allowed (if using offline packs).
  • Close other apps using the mic (voice memos, calls) and try again.

No transcript appears / Pauses randomly

  • Check Settings → Privacy → Speech Recognition and allow ThinkTask.
  • Some locales require network for speech; if offline, try enabling Enable Dictation in Settings → General → Keyboard.
  • If it stalls, tap Stop then Start again. You can also Import a voice note (.m4a) from Files.

“Go!” is disabled or AI fails

  • “Go!” requires an internet connection (Gemini).
  • If you’re offline, tap Save to queue the transcript and run “Go!” later.
  • If an error appears, copy it and email us with the steps to reproduce.

Import fails

  • Supported format: .m4a (MPEG-4 audio). Try re-exporting the file or placing it in iCloud Drive/On My iPhone first.
  • Very long files may take time to transcribe; keep the app in the foreground.

Confetti sound not playing

  • Ensure your device is not muted; increase volume. The app uses the ambient category and will mix with other audio.
