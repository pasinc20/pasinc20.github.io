Privacy Policy

Last updated: {today}

ThinkTask (“the App”) turns speech into an actionable checklist. This page explains what data the App handles, how it is used, and your choices. This policy is designed for the version of the App implemented in Swift Playgrounds as a single-file app using Apple Speech Recognition and the Google Generative Language (Gemini) API.

Summary (TL;DR)

  • No accounts and no in-app registration.
  • On-device storage only for your transcripts and plans; we (the developers) cannot access your data.
  • Audio stays on your device for transcription; the App does not send audio to Google. Apple’s Speech engine may process audio on-device or via Apple’s servers depending on availability.
  • When you tap Go!, the text transcript is sent to Google’s Generative Language (Gemini) API to generate a plan.
  • No ads, no third-party analytics, and we do not sell your data.

What the App Stores on Your Device

Your content is saved locally inside the iOS/iPadOS app sandbox:

  • Transcripts you type or dictate (ephemeral in the text box unless you save or generate a plan).
  • Offline queue entries you save manually.
  • Game plans (title, summary, checklist, notes, original transcript).

For resilience, the App writes to the Documents folder and mirrors to Application Support, plus a shadow copy in UserDefaults. Filenames include plans.v1.json and savedQueue.v1.json. Delete data in-app (swipe to delete / Clear All) or remove the App to erase its container.

Audio & Speech Recognition

  • The App uses the device microphone to capture speech for live transcription.
  • Transcription is performed by Apple’s SFSpeechRecognizer. Depending on your device and language, Apple may process speech on-device or via Apple’s servers. This behavior is controlled by iOS and your settings (e.g., “Enable Dictation” and “Improve Siri & Dictation”).
  • You may also import .m4a voice notes from Files for transcription. The App does not upload these files; they remain in your Files app and are processed through Apple’s speech framework.
  • The App itself does not retain or upload audio beyond transient processing for transcription.

AI Plan Generation (Google Gemini)

  • When you tap Go!, the App sends your text transcript (not audio) to Google’s Generative Language (Gemini) API to create a plan.
  • The request is made directly from your device to Google; there is no developer-operated server in between in this version.
  • Google processes the text to return structured steps. Processing may occur on servers outside your country. Please review Google’s applicable privacy terms for the Generative Language API.

What We Do Not Collect

  • No account data (name, email, phone).
  • No precise location, contacts, photos, or calendars.
  • No third-party analytics SDKs.
  • No advertising identifiers or ad-tracking.

Permissions

  • Microphone: to capture your voice for transcription.
  • Speech Recognition: to convert speech to text using Apple’s engine.
  • Files & Media (optional): when you choose “Import,” to select a voice note from the Files app.

Your Choices & Controls

  • Use the in-app delete actions to remove specific transcripts, offline items, or game plans.
  • Disable microphone or speech permissions in iOS Settings at any time.
  • To erase all local data, uninstall the App.
  • If you shared content to other apps (via the iOS share sheet), manage or delete that content within those apps.

Legal Bases (GDPR/UK GDPR)

  • Consent: microphone & speech recognition permissions.
  • Performance of a contract: to provide core App features you request (transcription, plan generation, saving items).

Children’s Privacy

The App is not directed to children under 13, and we do not knowingly collect personal data from children. If you believe a child provided personal data, please contact us so we can delete it.

Data Security

Data is stored locally in the App’s sandbox on your device. iOS provides filesystem protections (e.g., encryption when a device has a passcode). Keep your device updated and secured. Because processing with Google occurs directly from your device, any internet transmission is protected by TLS; however, no internet service is completely secure.

Data Sharing, Sale, and Advertising

  • We do not sell your personal data.
  • We do not share your personal data for cross-context behavioral advertising.
  • Third-party processing occurs only when you request AI plan generation (Google) or transcription via Apple Speech. Those parties process your data to provide their services.

International Transfers

Google may process the text you send to its AI service on servers located worldwide. By using the AI feature, you understand and consent to this transfer.

Retention

Your data persists on your device until you delete it in-app or uninstall the App. We do not host a server copy.

Changes

We may update this policy as the App evolves (e.g., if cloud sync, accounts, or analytics are added). We’ll post updates on this page and revise the “Last updated” date.

Contact

Questions or requests? Email support@yourdomain.com. If you are in the UK/EU and wish to exercise data rights (access, deletion, etc.), contact us at the same address.

Note: This policy describes the Swift Playgrounds build shown in the App’s code: local JSON persistence, Apple Speech for transcription, and Gemini API for plan generation. If you publish a version that adds cloud services, accounts, or analytics, update this page accordingly.
