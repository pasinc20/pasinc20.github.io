UK specs (Passport / DVLA)

  • Photo size: 35×45 mm (min 600×750 px at 300 dpi)
  • Head height: 29–34 mm (target 32 mm)
  • Neutral background: white / off-white / light grey
  • Even lighting, no harsh shadows
  • Eyes open, face the camera (child rules relaxed)

FAQ

Does the app upload my photos?

No. All processing (detection, background, export) is performed on your device.

Will my photo meet UK requirements?

The app guides you to the official head-size band, neutral background and lighting. Final acceptance is at the discretion of the issuing authority.

Can I print at home?

Yes—export the A4 sheet and print at 300 dpi on photo paper, then cut along the borders.

What devices are supported?

iPhone and iPad running iOS/iPadOS 16+. Live guidance uses your device’s camera in portrait.
