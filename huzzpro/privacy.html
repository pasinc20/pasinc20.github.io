Privacy Policy

Last updated: 15 August 2025

1. Who We Are

Subsly (“we”, “us”) provides the Huzz Pro iOS app and associated website. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information.

2. Information We Collect

  • Account Data: Optional display name and preference settings; device identifier.
  • Chat Content: Text you input, screenshots you upload, and any images you share with Huzz Pro for analysis.
  • Usage Data: Interaction events, crash logs, and basic diagnostics (no third-party ad trackers).
  • Purchase Data: Transaction IDs and subscription status from Apple In-App Purchase (we don’t receive card numbers).
  • Device Data: OS version, language, and timezone.

3. How We Use Your Data

  • Generate AI suggestions (including image/screenshot analysis) via supported AI providers (e.g., Google Gemini).
  • Improve quality, prevent abuse, and debug crashes.
  • Process subscriptions and provide customer support.
  • Send optional notifications if enabled by you.

4. Legal Bases

For users in the EEA/UK we rely on (a) contractual necessity to deliver the service, (b) legitimate interests to improve safety and performance, and (c) consent where required (e.g., notification permissions).

5. Sharing & Disclosure

  • Service Providers: Apple (App Store billing) and cloud/ML vendors needed to run features (e.g., Google Gemini). We may also use a privacy-friendly analytics provider (configure here if applicable).
  • Legal Requests: We disclose data if required by law or to protect rights and safety.
  • No Ads / No Sale: We do not sell or rent your personal information.

6. International Transfers

Data may be processed in countries where our providers operate. We rely on Standard Contractual Clauses or equivalent safeguards where required.

7. Data Retention

  • Content you submit persists only as long as needed to provide the feature or as required by law. You can request deletion.
  • Diagnostics/analytics may be kept for up to 24 months.
  • Backups may retain deleted data for ≤30 days.

8. Your Rights

You may have the right to access, correct, export, or delete your data, and to object or restrict certain processing. Contact us below to exercise your rights.

9. Children

Huzz Pro is not directed at children under 16. We do not knowingly collect personal data from minors under that age.

10. Security

We use TLS encryption in transit, encryption at rest, and least-privilege access controls. No system is 100% secure, but we work to protect your information.

11. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Policy. We’ll post changes here and, if significant, notify you in-app or by email.
