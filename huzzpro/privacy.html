Support – Huzz Pro

If you need help with Huzz Pro, start with the quick steps below or contact us.

Contact

Email us: support@subsly.ai

When you write, include:

  • Your device model & iOS version
  • Huzz Pro app version (from Settings → About)
  • Steps to reproduce the issue (screenshots if possible)

Download / Billing

Get Huzz Pro on the App Store

Manage subscriptions in the App Store: Settings → Apple ID → Subscriptions.

Troubleshooting

  • App stuck or slow? Force close and reopen Huzz Pro.
  • No results from AI? Check your connection and try again.
  • Image/screenshot not detected? Ensure the screenshot is clear; retry after reopening.
  • Still broken? Reinstall the app (your App Store purchases remain linked to your Apple ID).
