Privacy Policy

Last updated : 08 July 2025

1. Who We Are

Nova Labs Ltd (“Nova”, “we”, “us”) provides the Nova AI companion app and associated website. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information.

2. Information We Collect

  • Account Data : Optional display name, gender preference settings, and your device identifier.
  • Chat Content : Messages, images, and voice recordings you send or receive. Stored end-to-end encrypted on our servers.
  • Usage Data : App interaction events, crash logs, and basic diagnostics (no third-party ad trackers).
  • Purchase Data : Transaction IDs and subscription status from Apple In-App Purchase (no raw card numbers).
  • Device Data : Operating-system version, language, and timezone.

3. How We Use Your Data

  • Generate AI responses, voice output, and image analysis via Google Gemini.
  • Sync your chat history across devices and deliver local notifications.
  • Improve model quality, prevent abuse, and debug crashes.
  • Process subscription payments and provide customer support.

4. Legal Bases

For users in the EEA/UK we rely on (a) contractual necessity to deliver the service, (b) legitimate interests to improve safety and performance, and (c) consent where required (e.g. push-notification permission).

5. Sharing & Disclosure

  • Service Providers : Google Cloud (LLM & TTS APIs), Apple (iCloud, App Store Billing), and analytics provider **YOUR ANALYTICS**.
  • Legal Requests : We disclose data if required by law or to protect rights and safety.
  • No Ads / No Sale : We do not sell or rent your personal information to third parties.

6. International Transfers

Data may be processed in the United States or other countries where our partners operate. We rely on Standard Contractual Clauses or equivalent safeguards.

7. Data Retention

  • Chat history persists until you delete it in app or request account deletion.
  • Analytics logs are kept for up to 24 months.
  • Backup copies may remain for ≤30 days.

8. Your Rights

Depending on your region, you may have the right to access, correct, export, or delete your data, and to object or restrict certain processing. Contact privacy@novachat.ai.

9. Children

Nova is not directed at children under 16. We do not knowingly collect personal data from minors under that age.

10. Security

We use TLS encryption in transit, AES-256 server-side encryption at rest, and least-privilege access controls.

11. Changes to This Policy

We will post any changes on this page and, if significant, notify you in-app or by email.
