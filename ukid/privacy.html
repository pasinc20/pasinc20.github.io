Privacy Policy for UK Passport Photo Booth

Effective Date: 29 August 2025

UK Passport Photo Booth ("the App") is designed to help users create UK passport and ID-style photos that meet official GOV.UK requirements. Your privacy is extremely important to us. This Privacy Policy explains how we handle your information.

1. Data We Collect

- The App requires access to your device’s camera to take ID photos.

- All photos are processed locally on your device. They are only saved if you choose to save them.

- We do not collect, transmit, or store any personal information or photos.

2. How We Use Data

- Your photos are used only to generate compliant passport/ID images on your device.

- We do not use your data for advertising, analytics, or tracking.

3. Data Sharing

- We do not share your personal information with any third parties.

- No data leaves your device unless you choose to export or share it.

4. Children’s Privacy

- The App can be used by people of all ages.

- We do not knowingly collect any personal information from children.

5. Your Rights

As a user in the UK or EU, you have rights under the UK GDPR and Data Protection Act 2018, including the right to access or delete your data. Since the App does not collect or store data, there is nothing for us to delete.
