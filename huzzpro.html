Questions? We’ve got answers.

Is my conversation data stored?

Chats you paste stay on your device unless you choose to save them. You can clear history anytime, and saved threads are encrypted and synced securely via iCloud.

Can I create my own tone presets?

Yes. Start from Casual, Witty, or Bold — or create a custom instruction like “sound confident but relaxed”. Save it once and reuse it with one tap.

Does Huzz Pro work with screenshots?

Absolutely. Add up to four screenshots. We extract the conversation, highlight names, and give you context-aware options in seconds.

How do I cancel the subscription?

Open Settings > Apple ID > Subscriptions on your device. You can cancel anytime during the free trial or after — access continues until the current period ends.
